About the school

Education designed around unity, curiosity, and confidence.

Ekatva means unity. The school was founded to go beyond traditional education by nurturing children intellectually, emotionally, socially, and creatively.

The learning culture emphasizes strong moral values, play-based discovery, and thoughtful guidance so every student grows into a responsible and compassionate individual.
