About the School

Education Designed Around
Unity, Curiosity & Confidence

Ekatva means unity. Founded in 2024 under the Saraswathi Educational Academy in Ponnavaram, Nandigama, Ekatva EM School was built to go beyond traditional education — nurturing children intellectually, emotionally, socially, and creatively.

The learning culture emphasizes strong moral values, play-based discovery, and thoughtful guidance so every student grows into a responsible and compassionate individual ready for the world.

🎯

Our Vision

To nurture global citizens grounded in strong Indian values, equipped to lead with empathy and excellence.

🌱

Our Mission

Holistic education that blends academics, arts, sports, and character development in a safe, inclusive environment.

Academic Programs

A Clear Path from Early Learning to Confident Success

English medium instruction from pre-primary through senior school, built on a framework of holistic development.

Stage 1
🌟

WonderRoots Pre-Primary

A joyful foundational journey built on curiosity, play, expression, language, and early numeracy. Every child's first step into a lifelong love for learning.

  • Play-based learning
  • Early language development
  • Creative expression
  • Social & emotional growth
Stage 2
📖

Primary Years Grades 1 – 5

Concept-driven learning with steady skill building, classroom participation, and the development of critical foundational knowledge.

  • Core subjects mastery
  • Reading & writing skills
  • Mathematical thinking
  • Science exploration
Stage 3
🔬

Middle School Grades 6 – 8

Deepening knowledge with subject specialisation, project-based learning, and cultivation of independent thinking skills.

  • Subject specialisation
  • Project-based learning
  • Critical thinking
  • Leadership development

🎓

Senior School Grades 9 – 10

Critical thinking, exam readiness, and academic confidence for the future. Preparing students to excel in board examinations and beyond.

  • Board exam preparation

  • Analytical skills
  • Academic confidence
